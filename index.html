Tic Tac Toe

One player chooses an X as their symbol and the other player will have Os as theirs.

Use either the mouse or the arrow keys to move around the board. Click (or press Enter) to add your symbol in that square.

This is a tic tac toe game. You must have JavaScript installed in your browser to play it. The grid is 3 squares by 3 squares. Navigate the squares by pressing the left and right arrow keys and then press the 'x' key to play an x in the highlighted square or press the "o" key to play an o.
